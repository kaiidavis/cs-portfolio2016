Vindinium

Describing Vindinium

Vindinium is a multiplayer AI game where you code a bot to do certain functions. The goal of the game is to collect the most gold in the determined amount of turns. Each of the four players can take mines by defeating the goblin guarding it. When a player takes a mine, he becomes the owner and gains 1 gold per turn and has the goblin to guard his mine. Players can fight each other and killing a player lets you take all their mines. The killed player respawns at its spawn point with all its gold but no mines. There are taverns where you can purchase a drink with gold to heal your player. It is won by having the more intelligent bot that can win no matter what randomly generated map you are playing on by getting the most gold in the determined amount of turns.

The game shown above shows key aspects of the game, the turns being 1200 in this game, the taverns shown as creme soda, and the mines that are different colors showing who controls it.
